About MedVoyage

Leading Medical Tourism Company Based in Egypt

Connecting Global Patients with Egyptian Medical Excellence

MedVoyage is Egypt's premier medical tourism company, dedicated to serving African and global patients seeking world-class healthcare solutions. We specialize in coordinating comprehensive medical travel experiences with internationally accredited hospitals throughout Egypt.

From initial consultation to post-treatment follow-up, we provide seamless patient assistance including visa support, travel arrangements, accommodation, translation services, and 24/7 care coordination.

Mission

Seamless access to world-class medical care through personalized service and transparent pricing.

Vision

Africa's most trusted medical tourism partner through exceptional healthcare experiences.

Values

Trust, transparency, and patient-first coordination in everything we do.

Our Services

End-to-end coordination for your medical journey in Egypt

Visa on Arrival

Complete visa processing support including documentation, application assistance, and expedited services for medical travelers to Egypt.

Medical Consultation

Virtual and in-person consultations with Egyptian specialists, treatment plan reviews, and second medical opinions from expert doctors.

Hospital Booking

Direct appointments at top Egyptian hospitals with verified specialists, surgery scheduling, and comprehensive pre-admission coordination.

Accommodation

Premium hotels and recovery suites near medical facilities, including catering to dietary needs and mobility requirements.

Airport Pickup

Private transfers from Cairo International Airport to your accommodation with professional meet-and-greet services and luggage assistance.

Translation Services

Professional medical interpreters fluent in Arabic, English, French, and more to ensure clear communication with healthcare providers.
